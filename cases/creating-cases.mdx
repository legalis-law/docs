## Overview

Cases are the core of your legal practice management in Clerra. This guide will walk you through creating, organizing, and managing cases effectively.

## Creating a New Case

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Cases">
    Click **Cases** in the main navigation menu, then click the **New Case** button.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill in Basic Information">
    Enter the essential case details:

    <ParamField path="title" type="string" required>
      Descriptive name for the case (e.g., "Smith v. Johnson Contract Dispute")
    </ParamField>

    <ParamField path="case_number" type="string">
      Your internal reference number or court case number
    </ParamField>

    <ParamField path="case_type" type="select" required>
      Select from predefined types:
      - Civil Litigation
      - Criminal Defense
      - Corporate Law
      - Family Law
      - Real Estate
      - Intellectual Property
      - Employment Law
      - And more...
    </ParamField>

    <ParamField path="case_subtype" type="select">
      More specific categorization based on the selected case type
    </ParamField>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Client Information">
    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Existing Client">
        Select a client from your existing client list
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="New Client">
        Click **Add New Client** and enter:
        - Client name
        - Contact information
        - Business/individual type
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Assign to Department">
    If your workspace uses departments, assign the case to the appropriate legal department.

    <Info>
      Department assignment helps organize cases and control access for department-specific teams.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Additional Details">
    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="Description">
        Provide an overview of the case, including key facts and circumstances.

        <Warning>
          Sensitive descriptions are automatically encrypted for security.
        </Warning>
      </Accordion>

      <Accordion title="Preferred Outcome">
        Document the desired resolution or outcome for the case.
      </Accordion>

      <Accordion title="Other Parties">
        Add opposing parties, defendants, or other stakeholders involved in the case.
      </Accordion>

      <Accordion title="Representing">
        Specify who your firm is representing (plaintiff, defendant, etc.).
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Assign Team Members">
    Add attorneys, paralegals, and other team members to the case:

    - **Originating Attorney**: The attorney who brought in the case
    - **Assigned Attorneys**: Primary attorneys working on the case
    - **Team Members**: Paralegals, associates, and support staff

    <Tip>
      Team members will receive notifications and can access case documents based on their permissions.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Case">
    Review all information and click **Create Case** to finalize.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Case Information Fields

### Required Fields

| Field | Description | Example |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| Case Title | Descriptive name | "Acme Corp v. Beta LLC - Breach of Contract" |
| Case Type | Primary category | Civil Litigation |
| Client | Client name | Acme Corporation |

### Optional But Recommended

| Field | Description | Purpose |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| Case Number | Reference ID | Track internal/court numbers |
| Subtype | Specific category | Better organization and reporting |
| Description | Case overview | Quick reference for team |
| Department | Assigned dept | Department-level access control |
| Preferred Outcome | Desired result | Align team on goals |

## Case Status Options

When creating or updating a case, you can set the status:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Open" icon="folder-open">
    Active case in progress - default status for new cases
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="In Progress" icon="spinner">
    Work is actively being performed on the case
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="On Hold" icon="pause">
    Temporarily paused due to external factors
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Closed - Won" icon="trophy">
    Successfully resolved in client's favor
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Closed - Lost" icon="xmark">
    Unfavorable outcome
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Closed - Settled" icon="handshake">
    Resolved through settlement agreement
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Archived" icon="box-archive">
    Historical record keeping
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
