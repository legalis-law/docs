## Overview

Upload documents to Clerra's secure Vault system for case management and collaboration.

## Upload Methods

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Vault">
    Click **Vault** in the sidebar (not Documents)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Vault Type">
    - **Team Vault**: Shared with workspace
    - **Private Vault**: Personal documents
    - **Knowledge Base**: Firm templates and resources
  </Step>

  <Step title="Upload Files">
    Drag and drop or click Upload button
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Details">
    - Title, document type, case association
    - Folder location
    - Tags and metadata
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Maximum file size: 2GB. Supported formats: PDF, Word, Excel, images, and more.
</Info>
