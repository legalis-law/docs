## Folder Structure

Organize documents using hierarchical folders:

- **By Case**: Documents organized per case
- **By Client**: All documents for a client
- **By Type**: Contracts, Pleadings, Correspondence
- **By Date**: Monthly or quarterly organization

## Best Practices

- Use consistent naming conventions
- Tag documents for easy search
- Archive old documents regularly
- Review permissions quarterly
