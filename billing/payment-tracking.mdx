## Payment Tracking

Monitor invoice status and payments.

## Features

- Invoice status (Sent, Viewed, Paid, Overdue)
- Payment recording
- Partial payment tracking
- Automatic reminders
- Payment history
- Aging reports

## Payment Methods

- Paystack integration (GHS)
- Bank transfer
- Cash/check (manual recording)

<Info>
  Payment notifications sent automatically when invoices are paid.
</Info>
