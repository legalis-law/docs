## Invoice Creation

Create professional invoices from time entries and expenses.

## Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Billing">
    Go to case > Billing tab
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Entries">
    Choose time entries to invoice
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review Totals">
    Verify rates and calculate total
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate Invoice">
    Create invoice with firm template
  </Step>

  <Step title="Send to Client">
    Email invoice or share via portal
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Only case managers can setup billing. Invoices are in GHS (Ghanaian Cedi).
</Note>
