## Time Tracking

Track time spent on cases for accurate billing.

## Tracking Time

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start Timer">
    Click **Track Time** button anywhere in Clerra
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Case">
    Choose the case you're working on
  </Step>

  <Step title="Work">
    Timer runs while you work
  </Step>

  <Step title="Stop & Save">
    Stop timer and add description
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Features

- Real-time timer
- Manual time entry
- Bulk time entry
- Time entry templates
- Billable vs non-billable
- Department auto-tagging

<Tip>
  Time entries are automatically associated with your department.
</Tip>
