## Overview

The Clerra AI Chat Assistant is your intelligent legal companion, providing instant answers, document analysis, and strategic insights powered by advanced language models.

<Info>
  The AI Assistant is available on Pro, Teams, and Enterprise plans with varying token limits.
</Info>

## Accessing the AI Assistant

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click AI Icon">
    Find the AI Assistant icon in the top-right corner of the interface (robot or sparkle icon)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start Chatting">
    Type your question or request in the chat input field
  </Step>

  <Step title="Receive Response">
    Watch as the AI streams its response in real-time
  </Step>
</Steps>


```text Response
In California, a breach of contract claim requires four elements:

1. **Valid Contract**: There must be a valid contract between parties
2. **Performance**: Plaintiff performed their obligations or had excuse
3. **Breach**: Defendant failed to perform without legal excuse
4. **Damages**: Plaintiff suffered harm as a result of the breach

Key California cases include...
```

## Working with Case Context

When you have a case open, the AI Assistant automatically includes case context:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="With Case Context">
    ```text Question (with case open)
    What are the next steps in this matter?
    ```

    ```text Response
    Based on your case "Smith v. Johnson - Contract Dispute":

    **Immediate Next Steps:**
    1. Complete discovery responses (due in 14 days)
    2. Schedule expert witness depositions
    3. File motion for summary judgment

    **Timeline Considerations:**
    - Trial date: June 15, 2026
    - Discovery cutoff: March 15, 2026
    ...
    ```
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Without Case Context">
    ```text Question (no case open)
    What are the next steps in this matter?
    ```

    ```text Response
    I'd be happy to help with next steps! To provide specific guidance, could you tell me:

    - What type of legal matter is this?
    - What stage are you currently at?
    - Are there any upcoming deadlines?
    ...
    ```
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Document Analysis

Upload documents for AI-powered analysis:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Attachment Icon">
    In the chat interface, click the paperclip or attach icon
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Document">
    Choose a document from your computer or select from recent documents
  </Step>

  <Step title="Ask Questions">
    ```text
    Please summarize the key terms of this contract and highlight any potential issues.
    ```
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review Analysis">
    The AI will analyze the document and provide detailed insights
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Token Usage & Limits

Monitor your AI usage:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Usage">
    Go to **Settings > AI Usage** to view your token consumption
  </Step>

  <Step title="Monitor Limits">
    See your plan's monthly token allocation and current usage
  </Step>

  <Step title="Receive Alerts">
    Get notified at 80% and 95% of your token limit
  </Step>

  <Step title="Upgrade if Needed">
    Upgrade to a higher plan for increased token limits
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Plan Limits

| Plan | Monthly Tokens | Best For |
|------|---------------|----------|
| Free | 10,000 | Basic questions |
| Pro | 100,000 | Regular use |
| Teams | 500,000 | Team collaboration |
| Enterprise | Custom | Heavy usage |

<Info>
  Token usage depends on question length, response length, and context size. Complex analyses use more tokens.
</Info>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="AI Not Responding">
    - Check your internet connection
    - Verify you haven't reached token limit
    - Try refreshing the page
    - Contact support if issue persists
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Inaccurate Responses">
    - Provide more specific context
    - Include jurisdiction and relevant dates
    - Ask follow-up questions for clarification
    - Report systematic issues to support
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Token Limit Reached">
    - Wait until next billing cycle for reset
    - Upgrade to plan with higher limits
    - Optimize queries to use fewer tokens
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
