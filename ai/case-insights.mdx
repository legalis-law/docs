## Case Insights

AI analyzes your cases and provides strategic insights.

## Features

- **Case Summaries**: AI-generated overviews
- **Risk Assessment**: Identify potential risks
- **Strategic Recommendations**: Next steps
- **Similar Cases**: Find related matters
- **Timeline Predictions**: Estimated duration
- **Outcome Probability**: Success likelihood
- **Resource Planning**: Workload estimates

<Tip>
  Case insights improve as you add more case data and documents.
</Tip>
