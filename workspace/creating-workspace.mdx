## Overview

Create a workspace to start using Clerra for your law firm. Each workspace represents a separate legal practice with its own users, cases, documents, and billing.

<Info>
  Workspaces are created during the onboarding process after signing up for Clerra. The workspace creator becomes the first Super-Admin.
</Info>

## Workspace Creation Process

The workspace creation happens as part of the signup flow:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up">
    Navigate to https://app.clerra.app and enter your email to begin
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Account">
    Set up your user account with password
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Workspace">
    Fill in workspace details (see form fields below)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Plan">
    Choose your subscription plan
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Setup">
    Workspace is created and you're logged in as Super-Admin
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Workspace Creation Form

### Required Information

**Workspace Name** (Required)
- Your firm's name or practice name
- Must be unique across all Clerra workspaces
- Examples: "Mensah & Partners", "Accra Legal Associates"

**Super Admin Name** (Required)
- Your full name as the workspace administrator
- This becomes your display name in Clerra

**Subscription Plan** (Required)
- Pro, Teams, or Enterprise plan
- Free plans are not available
- Choose based on your firm size and needs

### Optional Information

**Practice Areas**
- Select the areas of law your firm practices
- Multiple selections allowed
- Helps configure relevant templates and playbooks
- Examples: Corporate Law, Litigation, Real Estate, Family Law

**Enable Case Billing**
- Toggle to enable/disable billing features
- Can be changed later in workspace settings
- Recommended for firms tracking billable hours

**Profile Image**
- Upload your profile photo
- Supported formats: JPEG, PNG, JPG
- Maximum size: 2MB
- Can be updated later in profile settings

**Team Members**
- Optionally invite team members during setup
- Enter email addresses of users to invite
- They'll receive invitation emails after workspace is created

### Subscription Plan Selection

During workspace creation, you select a subscription plan:

## What Happens After Creation

Once you create a workspace, Clerra automatically:

### 1. Sets Up Your Account
- Creates your user account
- Assigns you the **Super-Admin** role
- Grants all permissions for workspace management

### 2. Initializes the Workspace
- Creates workspace with your chosen name
- Sets up default departments (if applicable)
- Configures workspace settings
- Sets timezone to Africa/Accra (Ghana)

### 3. Creates Default Structure
- **Default Roles**: Super-Admin, Attorney, Paralegal, Member, Client
- **Default Folders**: Team vault, Private vault, Knowledge base
- **Templates**: Standard playbook templates based on practice areas

### 4. Sends Invitations
- Emails sent to any team members you invited
- Each receives a unique invitation link
- Links valid for 7 days

### 5. Starts Trial/Subscription
- 7-day trial period begins (if applicable)
- Or subscription activates if payment completed
- Access to all features based on selected plan

## Post-Creation Setup

After your workspace is created, complete these setup steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Verify Settings">
    - Go to Settings (click workspace name at top-right)
    - Review general settings
    - Confirm timezone (Africa/Accra, Ghana)
    - Add firm logo and branding
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Departments">
    - Navigate to Settings > Departments
    - Create departments for your practice areas
    - Assign department heads
  </Step>

  <Step title="Invite Team Members">
    - Go to Users, Roles, Rates
    - Invite additional team members
    - Assign appropriate roles and departments
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Billing">
    - Set default billing rates
    - Configure invoice templates
    - Set up tax settings for Ghana
  </Step>

  <Step title="Customize Templates">
    - Review and customize case templates
    - Set up document templates
    - Configure intake forms
  </Step>

  <Step title="Import Data (Optional)">
    - Import existing clients
    - Import active cases
    - Upload historical documents
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Multiple Workspaces

Depending on your plan, you may create multiple workspaces:

- **Pro Plan**: 3 workspaces
- **Teams Plan**: 10 workspaces
- **Enterprise Plan**: Unlimited workspaces

**Use Cases for Multiple Workspaces:**
- Separate office locations
- Different practice entities
- Client-specific workspaces
- Development/testing environments

<Warning>
  Workspaces are completely isolated from each other. Data cannot be shared across workspaces for security and compliance.
</Warning>

## Workspace Settings

Configure your workspace after creation:

### General Settings

**Workspace Information:**
- Name and description
- Logo and branding
- Contact information
- Physical address
- Timezone (default: Africa/Accra, Ghana)

**Business Hours:**
- Operating hours
- Weekend/holiday schedule
- Affects reminder timing and automation

### Security Settings

**Authentication:**
- Password policies
- Two-factor authentication requirements
- Session timeout duration
- Passkey authentication support

**Access Control:**
- Default permissions for new users
- Guest access policies
- Client portal settings

### Billing Settings

**Default Rates:**
- Attorney billing rates
- Paralegal billing rates
- Staff billing rates
- Currency: GHS (Ghanaian Cedi)

**Invoice Configuration:**
- Invoice templates
- Payment terms (NET 30, NET 60, etc.)
- Tax configuration for Ghana
- Late payment policies

**Payment Processing:**
- Payment provider: Paystack
- Supported payment methods
- Automatic payment reminders

### Department Configuration

Create and manage departments:
- Department name and description
- Department head assignment
- Member management
- Department-specific settings

## Workspace Limits

Each plan has different limits:

**User Limits:**
- Pro: Limited users per workspace
- Teams: More users per workspace
- Enterprise: Unlimited users

**Storage Limits:**
- Based on subscription plan
- Document storage capacity
- Email storage (if integrated)
- Backup retention period

**Feature Access:**
- AI token limits vary by plan
- Integration availability
- Custom branding options
- API access levels

<Info>
  Check your current usage and limits in Settings > Workspace > Usage Overview
</Info>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Workspace name already taken">
    - Choose a different, unique name
    - Add location or specialization to differentiate
    - Use full legal entity name
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Cannot complete subscription">
    - Verify payment method is valid
    - Check billing address is correct
    - Ensure sufficient funds/credit
    - Contact support if payment fails
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Invitation emails not sending">
    - Verify email addresses are correct
    - Check recipient spam folders
    - Resend invitations from Users section
    - Contact support if persistent
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Need to change workspace name after creation">
    - Go to Settings > General
    - Update workspace name
    - Changes reflect immediately
    - Note: URL slug may not change
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
