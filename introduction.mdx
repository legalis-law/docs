## What is Clerra?

Clerra is a modern, cloud-based legal practice management system designed to help law firms and legal professionals streamline their operations. From case intake to closure, billing to document management, and AI-powered insights to real-time collaboration, Clerra provides everything you need to run an efficient legal practice.

## Key Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="scale-balanced" title="Case Management">
    Comprehensive case tracking from intake to closure with automated workflows, timelines, checklists, and team collaboration.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="folder-open" title="Document Management">
    Secure document storage with versioning, organization, and powerful search capabilities. Organize files in team, private, or knowledge base vaults.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="clock" title="Time Tracking & Billing">
    Track billable hours, generate professional invoices, and manage payments with integrated billing workflows.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="sparkles" title="AI-Powered Intelligence">
    AI chat assistant, document analysis, case insights, and automated recommendations to enhance your legal work.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="users" title="Team Collaboration">
    Real-time notifications, comments, document collaboration, and shared workspaces for seamless teamwork.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="shield" title="Security & Compliance">
    Enterprise-grade security with encryption, audit logging, role-based access control, and compliance features.
  </Accordion>
