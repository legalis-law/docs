## What is Clerra?

Clerra is a modern, cloud-based legal practice management system designed specifically for law firms in Ghana. From client intake to case closure, billing to document management, and AI-powered insights to real-time collaboration, Clerra provides everything you need to run an efficient legal practice.

<Info>
  Access Clerra at **https://app.clerra.app**
</Info>

## Built for Ghana

- **Currency**: Ghanaian Cedi (GHS)
- **Timezone**: Africa/Accra (GMT)
- **Tax Compliance**: Ghana VAT and withholding tax support
- **Local Payment**: Paystack integration for GHS payments
