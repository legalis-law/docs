## Getting Started

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page at https://app.clerra.app, enter your email, and follow the reset instructions sent to your inbox.

**Q: How do I access Clerra?**
A: Navigate to https://app.clerra.app, enter your email and verification code, then use the magic link sent to your email to log in.

**Q: What timezone does Clerra use?**
A: Clerra is configured for Africa/Accra (Ghana Time) by default.

## Account & Workspace

**Q: Can I have multiple workspaces?**
A: Yes, depending on your plan. Pro plans include 3 workspaces, Teams includes 10, and Enterprise includes unlimited.

**Q: How do I switch between workspaces?**
A: Click your workspace name in the top-right corner and select a different workspace from the dropdown.

**Q: Where do I find workspace settings?**
A: Click the workspace name at the top-right corner to open the Settings modal.

**Q: How do I enable two-factor authentication?**
A: Click workspace name > Settings modal > Security tab > Enable 2FA

## Users & Roles

**Q: What's the difference between Admin and Super-Admin?**
A: "Admin" is for oversight/platform administration. "Super-Admin" is for workspace management and is the highest role for law firm users.

**Q: Can I create custom roles?**
A: Yes! Go to Users, Roles, Rates > Add New > Role, then configure permissions.

**Q: How do I invite team members?**
A: Navigate to Users, Roles, Rates > Invite Member, enter their details and assign a department.

**Q: Why must users be assigned to a department?**
A: Departments control access to cases and data. Every user needs a department assignment for proper access control.

## Cases & Matters

**Q: What happened to case timelines?**
A: Case timelines are deprecated. Use Checklists for all case workflow management.

**Q: How do I create a case?**
A: Navigate to Cases > New Case. Use AI-Assisted Intake for smart matter type identification.

**Q: Can cases be shared across departments?**
A: Yes, use the Share button on the case to grant cross-department access.

**Q: How do I set up billing for a case?**
A: Only the case manager (originating attorney) can setup case billing. This is an automatic permission for case managers.

## Documents & Vault

**Q: Where do I upload documents?**
A: Navigate to **Vault** in the sidebar (not "Documents"). The vault system manages all documents.

**Q: What file types can I upload?**
A: Most common file types are supported including PDF, Word, Excel, images, and more. Maximum file size is 2GB.

**Q: How do I organize documents?**
A: Use the folder structure in the Vault. Documents can be organized by Team, Private, or Knowledge Base vaults.

## Billing & Time Tracking

**Q: How is billing calculated?**
A: Billing is based on hourly rates set for each user role, multiplied by time entries logged to cases.

**Q: Where do I track my time?**
A: Click the Track Time button available throughout the app, or use the Time Tracking section.

**Q: Can I edit submitted time entries?**
A: Yes, you can edit your own time entries before they're approved. After approval, contact your case manager.

## AI Features

**Q: Where is the AI Assistant?**
A: Look for the AI Assistant floating panel at the bottom of every page in Clerra.

**Q: What can the AI help with?**
A: Legal research, document drafting, case strategy, contract review, and more. It's context-aware and can reference your cases.

**Q: Are there limits on AI usage?**
A: Yes, based on your subscription plan. Pro: 100,000 tokens/month, Teams: 500,000, Enterprise: custom.

## Support & Help

**Q: How do I get help if I'm stuck?**
A: - Check this FAQ and documentation at https://clerra.mintlify.app
- Email support@clerra.com
- Use the AI Assistant for quick questions
- Contact your workspace Super-Admin

**Q: Is there a mobile app?**
A: Clerra works on mobile browsers. Native mobile apps are in development.

**Q: How do I report a bug?**
A: Email support@clerra.com with details about the issue, including screenshots if possible.
