## Email Support

**Email**: support@clerra.com

**Response Times:**
- Pro Plan: Within 24-48 hours
- Teams Plan: Within 12-24 hours (priority support)
- Enterprise Plan: Within 4-8 hours (dedicated support)

**What to Include in Your Email:**
1. **Subject Line**: Brief description of issue
2. **Workspace Name**: Your firm/workspace name
3. **User Email**: Email address of affected user
4. **Issue Description**: Detailed explanation of the problem
5. **Steps to Reproduce**: How to recreate the issue
6. **Screenshots**: Visual documentation of the problem
7. **Browser/Device**: What you're using to access Clerra
8. **Urgency Level**: Critical, high, medium, or low

### Sample Support Email

```
Subject: Cannot Upload Documents to Case Vault

Workspace: Mensah & Partners
User Email: kofi.mensah@mensahpartners.com

Issue Description:
When attempting to upload PDF documents to the case vault for Case #2024-045, 
the upload fails with an error message "Upload failed - please try again."

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Navigate to Case #2024-045
2. Click on Vault tab
3. Click Upload Document
4. Select PDF file (under 5MB)
5. Click Upload button
6. Error appears

Screenshots: Attached

Browser: Chrome v120
Device: Windows 11 laptop
Internet Speed: 50Mbps

Urgency: Medium - blocking work on active case

Thank you!
```

## Escalation Process

For urgent or critical issues:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Contact via Email">
    Email support@clerra.com with "URGENT" in subject
  </Step>

  <Step title="Provide Details">
    Include all relevant information and mark urgency level
  </Step>

  <Step title="Initial Response">
    Receive acknowledgment within 1-4 hours (depending on plan)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Resolution">
    Work with support team to resolve issue
  </Step>

  <Step title="Follow-up">
    Confirm issue is resolved
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Critical Issues** (System down, data loss, security breach):
- Mark email as "CRITICAL - URGENT"
- Enterprise customers: Call dedicated support line
- Expect immediate response
