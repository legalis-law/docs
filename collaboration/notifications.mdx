## Notification System

Receive alerts for important updates and actions.

## Notification Types

- Case assignments
- Comments and mentions (@user)
- Document uploads
- Deadline reminders
- Approval requests
- Invoice updates
- Team invitations

## Channels

- **In-app**: Real-time notifications in Clerra
- **Email**: Digest or instant emails
- **Push**: Browser push notifications

## Settings

Configure notification preferences in your profile settings.
