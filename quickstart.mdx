## Welcome! Let's Get You Started

Follow this guide to set up your Clerra workspace and start managing your legal practice efficiently.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Clerra">
    Navigate to your workspace URL (e.g., `https://app.clerra.app`)

    **System Requirements:**
    - Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
    - Stable internet connection (5+ Mbps recommended)
    - No software installation required
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sign In">
    Enter your email and password, then complete two-factor authentication if enabled.

    <Tip>
      First time logging in? Check your email for the welcome message with your temporary password.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Your Profile">
    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="Add Your Information">
        - Full name and contact details
        - Profile photo (optional but recommended)
        - Timezone preferences
        - Notification settings
      </Accordion>

      <Accordion title="Enable Security Features">
        Go to **Settings > Security** and enable:
        - Two-factor authentication (2FA)
        - Passkey authentication (for passwordless login)
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Up Your Workspace">
    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Join Existing Workspace">
        If you received an invitation:
        1. Check your email for the invitation link
        2. Click **Accept Invitation**
        3. You'll be added to the workspace automatically
      </Tab>

      <Tab title="Create New Workspace">
        To create a new workspace:
        1. Click **Create Workspace** from the workspace switcher
        2. Enter workspace details (name, practice areas)
        3. Choose your subscription plan
        4. Configure initial settings
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Explore the Dashboard">
    Familiarize yourself with the main interface:

    <CardGroup cols={2}>
      <Card title="Navigation Menu" icon="bars">
        Access Cases, Documents, Time Tracking, and more
      </Card>
      <Card title="Dashboard" icon="home">
        View your assigned cases and recent activity
      </Card>
      <Card title="Notifications" icon="bell">
        Stay updated with real-time alerts
      </Card>
      <Card title="AI Assistant" icon="robot">
        Click the AI icon for intelligent help
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>
  </Step>
</Steps>
